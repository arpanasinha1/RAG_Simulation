RAG Simulator

Understand Retrieval → Augmentation → Generation

📖 Textbook (Knowledge Base)

1. Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year.

2. Sick leave is 10 days per year.

3. Parental leave is 12 weeks.

❓ Student Question

How many vacation days do employees get?

Step 1 — Retrieval

The system searches the documents and finds the most relevant sentence:

Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year.

Step 2 — Augmentation

The retrieved information is inserted into the prompt so the AI sees it together with the question. 

Context:
Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year.

Question:
How many vacation days do employees get?

Now the AI doesn't guess from memory. It uses the retrieved context.

Step 3 — Generation

The AI reads the context and generates the final answer.

Answer: Employees receive 20 paid vacation days per year according to the employee handbook.
